Deleting a Community

To delete a Community:

  1. In Security Design view, right-click on the Community and choose Delete.
  2. The Community is deleted from Rhythmyx.

    When you delete a Community, any associations of Rhythmyx elements with that Community are also removed. However, the elements are not deleted from Rhythmyx. Likewise, If Roles are assigned to Communities, the assignments are removed but the Roles are not removed from Rhythmyx.
