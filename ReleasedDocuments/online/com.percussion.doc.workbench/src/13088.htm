Field Transform Editor

The Field Transform editor lets you add an input or output transform to a field and maintain the transform.

To access the Field Transform editor:

  • When creating or editing a field in the Field Properties editor, click the [Transforms] button

The Field Transforms editor includes two tabs:

Input tab

Output tab
