Enabling and Disabling Content Types

When you enable a Content Type, you show it to users and let them access it. They can create, edit, delete, and search for Content Items of the Content Type. When you disable a Content Type, you hide it from users. They can neither create, edit, delete, nor search for Content Items of the Content Type.

The default FastForward Content Types are enabled by default. When you create a new Content Type, it is automatically enabled.

To disable a Content Type:

  1. In Content Design View, right-click the Content Type and choose Disable.

To enable a disabled content Type:

  1. In Content View, right-click the Content Type and choose Enable.
