Removing a Visibility Rule

Since a field visibility rule is a set of rules that are applied to a field, you do not delete the field visibility rule, but remove all of the visibility rules applied to the field.

For a graphic of the Field Visibility Rule editor and definitions of the fields discussed below, see Field Visibility Editor.

To remove a field visibility rule:

  1. In the Field Properties Editor, click [Visibility].

    The Visibility Rule editor opens.

  2. Click on each Rule in the Rule table and click .
  3. Click [OK].
