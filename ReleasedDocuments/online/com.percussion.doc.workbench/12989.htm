Community Visibility

Use the Rhythmyx Workbench's Community Visibility view to view or edit the objects that are visible to the members of a Community. The Community Visibility view lists all Communities on the Rhythmyx Server and displays the objects to which each Community has access.

To access the Community Visibility View, in the Menu bar, choose Window > Show View > Other. On the Show View dialog, choose Community Visibility.

Screenshot of Community Visibility View

The types of objects that can be visible to a Community are Content Types, Display Formats, Menus and Menu Entries, Searches, Sites, Templates and XSL Variants, Views, and Workflows. If you have modify ACL access to the object, you can drag and drop (or copy and paste) it from the following views to the Community which has access to it:
