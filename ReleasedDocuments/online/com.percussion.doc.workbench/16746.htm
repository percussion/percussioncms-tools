Allowed Templates Dialog

The Allowed Templates dialog allows you to specify the Templates associated with a Content Type. To access the Allowed Templates dialog, right-click on a Content Type and from the popup menu, choose Allowed Templates.

Screenshot of Allowed Templates Dialog

The Available Templates column lists all Templates available in the system that are not currently associated with the Content Type. Enter a string in the Filter field to filter the list of Templates to those beginning with the specified characters.

The Allowed Templates column lists all Templates currently associated with the Content Type.

Use the arrow buttons to move Templates between the two columns.
