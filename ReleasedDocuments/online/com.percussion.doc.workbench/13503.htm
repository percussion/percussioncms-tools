Maintaining Community Objects

A Community represents a group of roles that require access to similar information in Rhythmyx. A role can be associated with more than one Community, and users belong to all of the Communities associated with their roles.  

Rhythmyx will only grant users access to those Content Editors, Workflows, user interface components, Sites, and Templates that are associated with their Communities.

  • Use the Community Wizard to create new Communities.
  • Use the Community Editor to edit and maintain existing Communities.

You can:

  • Create a Community.
  • Edit a Community.
  • Delete a Community.
