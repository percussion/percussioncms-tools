Deleting a Keyword

To delete a keyword:

  1. In Content Design View, under the Keywords node, right-click the keyword and choose Delete.

    The keyword and its keyword choices are deleted.

    • Any field controls that are assigned the keyword are now assigned a blank keyword.
    • Any existing Content Items that have a field that stores one of the keyword choices will continue to store the keyword choice unless you open it in its Content Editor and save it.
