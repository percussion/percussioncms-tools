Removing a Field Validation

Since a field validation is a set of rules that are applied to a field, you do not delete the field validation, but remove all of the validation rules applied to the field.

For a graphic of the Field Validation editor and definitions of the fields discussed below, see Field Validation Editor.

To remove a field validation:

  1. In the Field Properties editor, click [Validation]

    OR

    from a Field and Field Sets editor, select a field and click [Validation].

    The Field Validation editor opens.

  2. Click on each Rule in the Rule table and click .
  3. Click [OK].
