Selecting a View

In the default Rhythmyx perspective, the CMS Navigator views and other Rhythmyx views open in a single window with tabs for accessing each view.

Screenshot of default Workbench window

You may have to widen the window to see the tabs for all of the views.

Screenshot of expanded Workbench window

To access the objects in a view, you can either click on its tab to make it the selected view, or choose it in the Menu bar.

To choose a view in the Menu bar:

  1. Choose Window > Show view.

    A sub-menu listing all of the CMS Navigator views and Other opens.

  2. Choose one of the CMS Navigator views or choose Other to open the Show View window which lists all available Rhythmyx views under the Rhythmyx CMS folder.

  3. Choose any view under Rhythmyx CMS to make it the selected view. The view opens if it is not shown in the default perspective.
