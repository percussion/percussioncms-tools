Modifying a Variant

To modify a Variant:

  1. Right-click on the Variant object that you want to modify and from the popup menu, choose Open.

    The Workbench displays the Variant Editor.

  2. You can modify any field or option.
  3. When you have finished making your modifications, click the [OK] button to save your changes.
