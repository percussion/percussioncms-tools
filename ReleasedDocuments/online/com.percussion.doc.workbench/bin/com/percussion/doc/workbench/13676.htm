Dragging and Dropping Menu Entries to Menus

To move menu entries into a menu:

  1. Drag and drop a Menu Entry from the Menu Entries folder to a Menu in the Menus folder. You can drag and drop from either System or User folders.

    A reference to the Menu Entry appears under the Menu.
