Viewing and Changing Log-In Status

The Account option in the Menu bar enables you to view and change your log-in information and to log out of Rhythmyx.

To view your log-in information, click Account > User Info in the Menu bar. The User Info dialog displays your log-in user, all of the user's Roles, and the Community and Locale under which you are logged in.

If your log-in user has more than one Community or Locale, the dialog displays the information as links. Click on either link to open a window that allows you to change your Community and/or Locale. After you change the Community or Locale, click [Go] to return to the previous page in Active Assembly under the new Community or Locale.

To log out of Rhythmyx, click Account > Logout.
