Removing a Snippet from a Slot

You can remove a Snippet from a Slot if the page that holds the Slot is not Public and is checked out to you.

You can access a remove option either when a specific Snippet is selected or when an entire Slot is selected.

To remove an item selected in a Slot:

  1. Select the Snippet in the Slot.
  2. In the Menu Bar click Edit > Remove.

OR

  1. Right-click on the Snippet in Outline view or on its icon in Page view and choose Remove.

    The Snippet is removed from the Slot.

To select a Slot and then choose which items to remove:

  1. Right-click on the Slot in Outline or Page view.

    Rhythmyx displays a menu of Snippet options.

  2. Click Remove Snippets.

    Rhythmyx opens the Remove Snippets from the Slots dialog. If the assembled Snippets are large and extend beyond the window in the dialog, you can click [Show Titles] to just view the title of each Snippet. Clicking [Show Titles] in the following example is not necessary because there are only two assembled Snippets, and each one only fills one line.

  3. Click the Snippet(s) that you want to remove or click [Select All] to select all Snippets for removal.

    If you decide not to remove a Snippet that you have selected, click on the Snippet again to deselect it, or to start over click [Deselect All].

    After you select one or more Snippets, the [Remove] button is enabled.

  4. Click [Remove].
  5. Rhythmyx closes the Remove Snippet(s) from the Slot dialog closes and removes the item(s) from the Slot.
