Dates in Internationalized Content

Translation Copies may require dates to have different formats than their owner Content Items. For example, a Content Item in English could have the date format mm/dd/yyyy while its Translation Copy in French could have the date format dd/mm/yyyy. Since the actual content in the field is different, if you perform a search on the date, you must enter the correct date format for the Locale of the Content Item if you want a search to return it as a result.

The implementers of your CMS may add functionality to the search component to convert dates that you enter to match the Locale of the content they are searching. In this case, entering a date in your default Locale's format will return Content Items with different date formats.
