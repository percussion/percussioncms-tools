Accessing a URL Link

The menu bar's Tools > Link to Page option lets users copy the main content item's URL or a Snippet's URL and paste it as a link into another application such as IM or an email application. Rhythmyx may also send a Content Item URL link in an email message sent during a Workflow Transition.

If you are logged in to a third-party application that includes a Content Item URL link, you may click on the link to access the Content Item.

To access a Rhythmyx Content Item through a link in a third-party application:

  1. Click on the link.

    If you have not yet logged in to Rhythmyx, a dialog prompts you for your username and password. Rhythmyx logs you in with the same Community as the Content Item, or your default Community, if you are not a member of the Content Item's Community.

    Note that this action functions slightly differently than the same action in Content Explorer. Since the link in Active Assembly includes the site id and folder id, if the item is in more than one site or folder, you do not have to choose the site or folder to access.

  2. The item opens as the main item in Active Assembly.
