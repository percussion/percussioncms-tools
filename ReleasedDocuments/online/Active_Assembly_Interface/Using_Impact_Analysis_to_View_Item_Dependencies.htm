Viewing Relationships

The Impact Analysis window displays the owners and dependents of a Content Item as well as their owners and dependents (referred to as ancestors and descendants of the Content Item). You do not have to check out a Content Item to view its dependencies.

The Impact Analysis window shows descendant and ancestor trees for the owner Content Item for the relationship that you choose in the drop list at the top of the window.  The listing includes the name of the item, the user who has checked it out, its current State, and its Content Type

To view the dependents of a Content Item:

  1. Select the main item or a Snippet in a Slot.
  2. In the menu bar, choose Tools > Impact Analysis.

    Rhythmyx opens the Impact Analysis window for the Content Item. By default Active Assembly is selected in the Relationship drop list.

  3. Choose the name of the Relationship whose descendant and ancestor trees you want to view in the Relationship drop list. By default, the drop list includes all of the Relationships in your Rhythmyx system.

    The Descendents and Ancestors boxes display the Descendent and Ancestor trees for the content item in the Relationship.

  4. To view the Descendent and Ancestor trees for the Content Item in another Relationship, choose the Relationship in the Relationship drop list.
  5. To refresh the window so that it displays ancestors and descendants created since you opened it, in the Menu Bar, choose View > Refresh.
  6. To change the appearance of the window, choose View > Options, and set new options.
