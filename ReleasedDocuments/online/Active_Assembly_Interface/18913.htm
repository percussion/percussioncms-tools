Replacing a Snippet with Another Snippet

Active Assembly allows you to choose a Snippet in a Slot that you want to remove and either create a new item or choose an existing item to insert in its place.

To create a new item and replace an existing Snippet with it:

  • Follow the instructions in Creating a New Content Item and Adding it to a Slot, but in the Choose Where to Place Snippet dialog always choose the Replace radio button under Place Where.

To choose an existing item and replace an existing Snippet with it:

EITHER

  • Follow the instructions in Inserting Existing Content Into Slots, but in the Choose Where to Place Snippet dialog always choose the Replace radio button under Place Where.

OR

  1. Right-click on the original Snippet in Outline view or Page view and choose Replace.

    Rhythmyx displays the Choose Where to Place Snippet dialog.

  2. Follow step 3 through step 5 in the topic Inserting Existing Content Into Slots.
