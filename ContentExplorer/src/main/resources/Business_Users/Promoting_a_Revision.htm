Promoting a Revision

If you need to replace the current revision of a Content Item with an earlier revision, you can promote the earlier revision. When you promote a revision, Rhythmyx checks the revision out to you and makes it the current revision.

To promote a revision:

  1. In the Action Menu, select View > Revisions.

    Rhythmyx displays the Revision List.

  2. Click the Action Menu arrow in the row of the revision that you want to promote.

    NOTE: If the Action Menu does not display the Promote option, the item may be checked out or you may not have the access necessary for promoting the item.

  3. Select Promote in the Action Menu.

    Rhythmyx checks the Content Item out to you and makes the promoted revision the current revision.
