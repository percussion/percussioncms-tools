Performing a Transition on a Content Item

To perform a Workflow transition from the Action Menu:

  1. From the Action menu, select Workflow > Transitions > [transition name]. If the Content Item is not already checked in, Rhythmyx checks it in.

    Rhythmyx displays a pop-up for entering Workflow Comments and any other controls for performing the transition.

     

  2. If Rhythmyx displays a Workflow Comments field with a red asterisk, comments are required.  Enter a comment.

    If Rhythmyx displays a Workflow Comments field without an asterisk, comments are optional.  Optionally, enter a comment.

  3. If Rhythmyx displays an Ad Hoc Assignees field, optionally search for and assign an Ad Hoc assignee.
  4. Click the transition button to complete the transition.

    If the transition is configured to send notifications, it may send an email to certain users notifying them that the transition has occurred.

If additional Roles are required to perform the Transition, you will no longer see the Transition available under Workflow in the Action menu, but the Content Item's State will not change until all required Roles have transitioned it.

If your log-in Community is different from the Community of the Content Item, Check-in and Check-out are the only Workflow Transitions available to you.
