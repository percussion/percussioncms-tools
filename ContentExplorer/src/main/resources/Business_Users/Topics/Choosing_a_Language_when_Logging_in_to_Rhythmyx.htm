Choosing a Language when Logging in to Rhythmyx

Your Locale represents the language and dialect of the text on your Rhythmyx interface. If your Locale is French Canadian, the link names, tabs, menu options and other text on your interface is in French Canadian; if your Locale is American English, these components have American English labels.

The language of the text within a Content Item does not change depending on the Locale. To have a version of a Content Item in another language, you must create a Translation Copy with a separate Content ID and translate the text manually.

After you log in to Rhythmyx, Rhythmyx displays a page with a Locale drop list if:

  • Locales are used in your system.
  • You have more than one Locale.

You can also access the page with the Locale drop list from Content Explorer by clicking the Locale link at the top of the page.

Rhythmyx displays the Locale drop list on the Community Log In page if you must also select a Community.

The Select a Language drop list lists all of your Locales.   When you choose the name of the Locale under which you want to log in, Rhythmyx transfers you to your Rhythmyx Home Page.
