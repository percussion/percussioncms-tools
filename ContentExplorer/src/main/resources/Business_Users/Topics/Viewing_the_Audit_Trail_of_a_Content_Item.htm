Viewing the Audit Trail of a Content Item

The Audit Trail lists transitions for the revisions of the active item. To view the Audit Trail of the active item, click the Action Menu link and choose View > Audit Trail.

The Audit Trail displays the following columns:

  • Date - Date of the transition.
  • Transition (id) - The transition.
  • Rev - Revision of item on which the transition occurred.
  • To State(id) - State of item after transition.
  • Publishable - "yes" if item was publishable after transition; "no" if item was not publishable after transition.
  • Who - User who performed the transition.
  • Comment - Link to a comment associated with the transition.

At the top of the Audit Trail page is a link to the Revision List.
