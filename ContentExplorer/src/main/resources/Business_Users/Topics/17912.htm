Range Queries

Range queries find content items with field values that fall within a specified range. Range queries can be inclusive or exclusive of the upper and lower bounds of the range. Use square brackets for inclusive range queries and curly brackets for exclusive range queries.

for example, in Search for, enter:

mod_date:[20020101 TO 20030101]

finds documents whose mod_date fields have values between 20020101 and 20030101, inclusive.

You can also use range queries with non-numeric fields. for example, in Search for, enter:

title:{Aida TO Carmen}

finds documents whose titles are between Aida and Carmen, but not Aida nor Carmen.

Search results are returned in dictionary order.
