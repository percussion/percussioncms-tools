Refreshing the Page

Depending on your Rhythmyx configuration, after you add or modify information in Content Explorer, the page may refresh itself, or you may have to manually refresh it to view changes. You can refresh the entire page, or only refresh a specific Folder.

To refresh an entire page, click the refresh button at the top of the View:

You can also refresh the View from the Menu bare by choosing View > Refresh. Content Explorer now displays your most recent changes.

To manually refresh a specific Folder:

  1. In the Navigation Tree, select the folder to refresh.
  2. In the Menu Bar, choose Content > [folder name] > Refresh.

    The folder now displays your most recent changes.

OR

  1. In the Navigation Tree, right-click the folder to refresh.
  2. In the drop menu, choose Refresh.

    The folder now displays your most recent changes.
