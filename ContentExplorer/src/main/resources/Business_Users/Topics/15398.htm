How to Remove Dependent Items and Reset Active Assembly Links

To remove a Content Item from a Site folder in the following situations you must use a Force Remove action to reset Active Assembly links:

  • If a Content Item is a dependent in an Active Assembly Relationship, and its siteid and folderid are properties of the Relationship.
  • If a Content Item is a dependent in an Active Assembly Relationship, and its siteid is a property of the Relationship, and no other instances of the Content Item appear in other Folders on the Site.
  • If a Content Item is a dependent in an Active Assembly Relationship, and its folderid is a property of the Relationship (and its siteid is not a property of the Relationship).

To force a remove:

  1. You must be in a Role and Community that has access to the Force Remove action. By default, administrators in administrator Communities have access to the Force Remove action.
  2. In the Content Explorer display pane, right-click on the item and choose Force Remove.
