Accessing the Active Assembly Interface

In Content Explorer, you can access the Active Assembly interface in several different ways:

  • You can open Active Assembly for a content item by right-clicking on the content item and selecting Active Assembly in the Action Menu.
  • You can also open Active Assembly for a content item by opening the content item in its content editor and choosing one of the related content links at the bottom of the content editor.
  • You can access the Active Assembly interface for an entire site through the Site Explorer drop menu.
