Managing Active Assembly in Expert Mode

To manage related content from a Content Editor open the Content Item in the Content Editor and click [Edit All] at the bottom of the form. This opens the Editor Assembly window. The Editor Assembly window includes action buttons  for managing related content.   

To change the position of a Related Content Item:

  • In the row of the Content Item whose position you want to change,
    click the promote or demote button .

To change the Slot or Template of a related Content Item:

  1. Click the Modify Item Slot/Template button :

    Rhythmyx displays the Modify Item Slot/Template page.

  2. From the drop lists, select the new Slot or Template for the item.
  3. Click [Update].

To remove a related Content Item from Slot:

  • In the Related Content table, click the remove button in the row of the item you want to remove.

To display a related Content Item in the Content Editor in view mode:

  • Click the display button .

To see a preview of a formatted Template of the Content Item:

  • Click the name of the item.
