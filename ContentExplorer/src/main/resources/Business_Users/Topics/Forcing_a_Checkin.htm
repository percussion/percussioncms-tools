Forcing a Check In

Administrators have the Force Check-in option available in the Workflow > Transition sub-menu of their Action Menus. The Force Check-in option lets administrators check in an item that is checked out by another user. Only edits that the user has saved are applied when the Administrator checks in the Content Item.
