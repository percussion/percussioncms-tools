Proximity Searches

Lucene supports finding words in a phrase that are within a specific distance away from each other. To do a proximity search use the tilde, "~", symbol at the end of a Phrase. For example to search for "apache" and "jakarta" within 10 words of each other in a document, in Search for, enter:

"jakarta apache"~10
