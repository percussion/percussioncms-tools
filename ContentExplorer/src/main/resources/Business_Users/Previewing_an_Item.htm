Previewing an Item

Content Explorer lets you preview Content Items in different Templates through the Preview option in the Action Menu for the Content Item.

To preview a Content Item in a Template from the Action Menu, right-click the Content Item in the Display pane and choose Preview > [Template] in the Action Menu.

You cannot edit the Content Item in the preview Template, but you can follow links to view related Content Items.
