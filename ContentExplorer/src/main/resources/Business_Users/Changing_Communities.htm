Changing Communities

If you are a member of more than one community, you can change your log-in community by clicking the Community value at the top of Content Explorer. After you change your Community, Content Explorer displays only the Content Editors, workflows, Templates, and screen components available to the selected Community.

To change your Community:

  1. At the top of Content Explorer, click the Community.

    Rhythmyx displays the Community Login Page.

  2. Choose a new Community in the Community drop list.

    Rhythmyx changes your login Community and transfers you back to Content Explorer.
