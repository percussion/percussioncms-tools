Changing Locales

If you have more than one Locale, you can change your log-in Locale by clicking the Locale value at the top of Content Explorer. After you change your Locale, the Content Explorer and Site Explorer display labels in the language that corresponds to your Locale.

To change your Locale:

  1. At the top of Content Explorer, click the Locale value. (If you only have the default Locale (US English), Content Explorer does not display it.)

    Rhythmyx displays the Community Login (and Locale login) page.

  2. Select a new Locale in the Locale drop list.

    Rhythmyx changes your Locale and transfers you back to Content Explorer.
