Views

Views display content in the Display pane sorted and selected by predefined parameters. Views are useful because they arrange content by categories that would be difficult to maintain in folders, such as Workflow States and assigned users, and they allow you to see Content Items that are not stored in your folders. However, you cannot change the views available to you; they are predefined in your CMS.

The default views in the Navigation pane are:

My Content - Filters content by user, content status, and creation date in one of the following sub-Views:

  • Checked Out By Me

    Displays content that is checked out to you.

  • Inbox

    Displays content that is assigned to you.

  • Outbox

    Displays content that you have created.

  • Recent

    Displays all content assigned to you or added/modified by you within the last 48 hours.

  • Session

    Displays all content assigned to you or added/modified by you during the current login session.

Community Content - All content in user's Community.

All Content - All content.

Other Content - Content that does not match any other view.

You may see labeledicons below Views. These are not additional Views, but Categories for displaying Content Items in a View. Categories are part of the View's Display Format.
