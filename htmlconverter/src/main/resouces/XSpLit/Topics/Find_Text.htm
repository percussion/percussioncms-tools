Find Text

Use the Find dialog to find text you specify. To access the Find dialog,

choose Edit > Find; or

type CTRL-F .

Enter the text you want to find in the Find what box and click Next or press <Enter>.

Check Match case to make your search case-sensitive.

To continue a search from the current cursor position, press F3. (If you have not already defined a search, F3 will open the Find dialog.)

To close the dailog,

click [Cancel]; or

press <Escape>.
